Game Projects

Mario Wack The Mole

The object of this game is to click on the mole as many times as possible without clicking on The Piranha Plant. Your score increases with each click on the mole, offering a delightful challenge and encouraging players to aim for higher scores. The fast-paced nature of the game adds an element of excitement, testing players' reflexes and precision. However, one must exercise caution, as a single click on The Piranha Plant brings the game to an end. This simple yet engaging game was crafted in just 30 minutes, showcasing the power of creativity and quick development. It stands as a testament to the joy found in uncomplicated, spontaneous gaming experiences that captivate and entertain players of all ages.

Flappy Bird

In Flappy Bird, the objective is to navigate a small bird through a series of pipes by tapping the screen or pressing a key to make the bird flap its wings. The challenge lies in timing these flaps to avoid collision with the pipes. The goal is to achieve the highest possible score by passing through the maximum number of pipe gaps. The game tests players' reflexes, concentration, and precision as they strive to keep the bird airborne and navigate through the increasingly challenging pipe arrangements. The simplicity of the objective contributes to the addictive nature of Flappy Bird, offering a straightforward yet challenging gaming experience.

Find The Ace --Luck Based--

The objective of the game is to find the ace among four displayed cards. These cards are shuffled, and your task is to follow the ace as the cards move rapidly, testing your ability to keep track and make a quick decision. Rooted in luck and the art of intuitive guessing, the game challenges players to trust their instincts and make split-second choices. The dynamic nature of the shuffling cards adds an element of unpredictability, making each round a thrilling and chance-driven experience. It's a game that invites players to embrace the excitement of uncertainty, providing a simple yet engaging platform to hone their intuition.

Tic-tac-Toe

The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to be the first player to create a line of three of their symbols (either X or O) in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row on the game grid. Players take turns placing their symbols on an empty spot on the grid, strategically aiming to block their opponent from forming a winning line while simultaneously working to create their own. The game ends when one player successfully aligns three of their symbols in a row, or when the entire grid is filled without a winner, resulting in a draw. Tic Tac Toe is a classic and straightforward game that requires strategic thinking and careful planning to outmaneuver the opponent.

Connect4

The objective of Connect 4 is to be the first player to connect four of their colored discs in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the game grid. Players take turns dropping one of their discs into any of the seven columns, causing it to occupy the lowest available space in that column. The challenge lies in strategically placing discs to create a connected line of four, while also strategically blocking your opponent from achieving the same. The game requires a blend of foresight, planning, and adaptability, making it a classic and engaging two-player experience.
